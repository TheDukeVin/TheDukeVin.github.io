Kevin Du's Climate Story

How to play

Press the arrow keys to move the characters. Press 1 and 2 keys to switch between the characters. The goal is to get all characters into the green zone.

About

This game was made as a project for Earth Day it is an adaptation of my original game, found at my website . It is based on teamwork and collaboration between different people aligned by a common goal, which is the philosophy behind earth day. Happy Earth Day everyone!
